About

RalphVR, Inc. is a virtual reality studio founded in 2015 to develop virtual reality applications that afford new ways to: connect with one another, share new experiences together, and lift up our vulnerable through technology. Our first experience is Daydream Blue, which is available on GearVR, Oculus Rift, and Google Daydream. Currently we are hard at work on something very exicitng, with an incredibly talented group of folks--across many disciplines--at the University of Kentucky. We will have exciting announcements soon!
